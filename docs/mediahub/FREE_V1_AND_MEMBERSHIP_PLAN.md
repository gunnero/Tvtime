# Free V1 And Future Membership Plan

## Principle

MediaHub must first be excellent as a free, private tracking product. V1 contains no subscription code, payment integration, entitlement checks, trials, upgrade prompts, or artificial limits.

## Free V1

Every account receives:

- tracking and permanent watch history
- discovery and canonical library management
- movie and show watchlists
- ratings and private notes
- entertainment diary
- trustworthy statistics
- release calendar and in-app alerts
- private manual lists
- TV Time import
- JSON and practical CSV exports
- metadata enrichment and status

User data ownership is not a premium feature.

## Possible Future Plus

Only after V1 quality and product fit are proven, a future membership may add:

- Kalveri AI recommendations and insights
- smart collections
- advanced statistics
- family profiles
- native app enhancements
- cross-service availability
- automation
- premium backup options

These ideas are not approved implementation scope. Canonical history, manual tracking, import, export, privacy, and basic statistics should remain useful without payment.

## Future Decision Gates

Before membership work begins, require:

1. stable V1 data accuracy
2. measured use of discovery, calendar, stats, and lists
3. a written entitlement model
4. privacy and consumer-law review
5. clear free-tier permanence
6. a separate payment-security design

No feature gate should be introduced opportunistically inside an unrelated sprint.
